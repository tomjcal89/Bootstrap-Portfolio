Welcome!

Hello! My name is Thomas I was born and raised here in the Bay Area. Some people refer to me as the insurance guy. I currently work at State Farm (and no, I do not wear khakis). I first got into insurance when I moved to Austin, Texas in 2015 where I started to work for Progressive (no, I don’t know Flo, but I did take a picture with her cardboard cutout once). Before I moved to Austin, I was a Customer Service Manager at Old Navy in Downtown San Francisco. I did that for about 5 years, and let me tell you, retail is brutal.

Something unique about myself I would say is that I have a blackbelt in Tae Kwon Do. Now, I got it when I was 13 and haven’t practiced it since I was 14, so I wouldn’t say I know much anymore. But, seeing how I do have a blackbelt, its has always a fun little fact to share with people.

I have always been fascinated with coding and how it makes everything work. I am ecstatic to be in this class and to finally get my hands dirty! There have been a lot of moving parts on where I am today, and I can say this class is the next step to get me where I want to be. Once I graduate from the boot camp, I will be on the lookout for an industry and job switch to one in which I can showcase my new skillsets. .

This journey is going to be one wild rollercoaster ride and I cannot wait to see where it takes me!
